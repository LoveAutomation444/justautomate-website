The Background

Engineering Meets Real-World Problem Solving

Before starting Just Automate, the work was deep in the weeds of QA automation frameworks, AI model testing pipelines, and DevOps infrastructure. That background meant one thing: an obsession with systems that actually work, not just systems that look good in a demo.

The spark for Just Automate came from watching everyday problems go unsolved. School pickup lines running on paper clipboards. Real estate agents buried in email threads tracking which contracts got signed. Clinic staff spending their evenings writing notes instead of caring for patients.

These aren't enterprise problems. They're human problems, and they all have automation solutions that most agencies are too focused on big clients to build.

That's the gap Just Automate fills: practical, affordable, elegant automation for the businesses and organizations that need it most.
